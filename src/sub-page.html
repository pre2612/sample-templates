About Us

M & K Solicitors is a law firm based in Luton. The firm was set up in January 2007 and has since expanded rapidly. The firm specialises in all aspects of Immigration, Administrative law, Employment, Personal Injury, Family, Property and Civil Law. The firm has private clientele from all around the country due to its excellent reputation in providing a high quality service.

The team at M & K solicitors are approachable, experienced and dedicated to providing an outstanding service.

The staff at M & K Solicitors are multilingual and can speak a variety of languages including English, Urdu, Hindi, Punjabi, Pahari, Tamil and Malayalam.

We understand that every client is unique and strive to meet their individual needs to a high standard
